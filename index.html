The National Vocational & Technical Training Commission (NAVTCC) is Pakistan's premier government authority dedicated to standardizing and promoting technical education and vocational training across the country.

This AI Portfolio represents NAVTCC's commitment to integrating Artificial Intelligence into its core curriculum and national digital transformation agenda — training the workforce of tomorrow.

Through strategic partnerships with universities, technology companies, and research institutions, NAVTCC is cultivating a generation of AI-literate professionals who can compete on the global stage.

Projects showcased here are built by NAVTCC-certified students and researchers — proof that world-class AI development is happening right here in Pakistan.
